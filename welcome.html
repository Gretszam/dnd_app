Welcome...

to Dungeons and Dragons (D&D) Fifth Edition (5e) Character Tracker! This is an app to help D&D players keep track of their characters' stats and be able to level up their characters for free. A charactersheet on-the-go! Like a physical charactersheet you will need to ask your Dungeon/Game Master (DM/GM) or reference the Player Handbook in order to fill out information you do not know or would like more information on.

You can access other pages through the links at the bottom of each page. Said links will be one-word descriptions of what you will find there. To start out click the "Stats" link and decide who your next advventurer will be. Have fun!
